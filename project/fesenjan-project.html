Fesenjan

My favorite Iranian stew

Fesenjan is one of the authentic Iranian stews from northern Iran. It is typically served over rice in the Iranian manner. In cooking this stew walnut kernels and pomegranate paste are used together with chicken or red meat and it is served over rice. Due to the various ingredients used in this stew, Fesenjan can have many different forms. Also, because the cooking process of this food was long and difficult in the past and its ingredients were not available to everyone, this food was considered as royal food. Although its aristocracy has been redused today, it is still referred to as an elaborate food.
